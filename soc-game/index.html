// HOW TO PLAY — PumaSOC
OBJECTIVE

Survive 7 game days as operator of a global Security Operations Center. Defend your client estate against nation-state and criminal threat actors. You lose if 3 breaches succeed.

THE THREAT LOOP

Threat actors continuously probe your estate. Each attack is checked against your Detection Coverage %. If detected, an incident appears and you must respond. If not detected, the threat dwells silently — and becomes a BREACH after ~2 real minutes undetected.

A detected incident left unassigned for ~4 minutes also breaches.

RESPONDING TO INCIDENTS
  1. Alert fires in the SIEM strip at the bottom
  2. Incident appears in the center panel
  3. Click ASSIGN ANALYST to start the IR clock
  4. Analyst works through: TRIAGE → CONTAIN → ERADICATE → RECOVER
  5. Resolved incidents pay a small budget bonus

You start with 2 analysts. Each handles one incident at a time.

NETWORK MAP

Shows your client estate. Node colours change as threats land:

  • ● Green — healthy
  • ● Amber — known vulnerabilities present
  • ● Red — under active attack

Moving red packets on the map are threats in transit to a target.

SIEM FEED

The strip at the bottom shows the last 8 events. Click it to expand the full scrollable history. CRIT alerts need immediate attention.

YOUR CAPABILITIES
DETECTION ENG Invest in SIEM rules per ATT&CK category. Each level raises your overall Coverage % — the single most impactful stat. Raise this first.
VULN MGMT Scan assets to find CVEs, then patch them. Lower Vuln Score means attacks succeed less often and take longer to land.
RED TEAM Commission a pentest. Takes time but reveals hidden detection gaps and undiscovered vulnerabilities your defenders missed.
THREAT INTEL Subscribe to intel feeds for passive boosts to Coverage % and Mean Time to Detect (MTTD).
HIRE ANALYST More analysts = more incidents handled simultaneously. Essential as threat tempo escalates. Max 8.
CONSULTANT Retains an expert who adds a passive defensive bonus and delivers a daily briefing — one prioritised recommendation telling you the single best action to take next.
KEY STATS
  • Coverage % — chance of detecting any attack. Your most important number.
  • MTTD — Mean Time to Detect. Lower = you see threats sooner.
  • Vuln Score — attack surface exposure. Lower is better.
  • Analysts — busy / total available responders.
CONTROLS
